# Safety rules

1. Do not execute a write without a validated change plan.
2. Do not trust model-generated identifiers without fetching the object.
3. Do not modify an account outside the configured allowlist.
4. Do not combine unrelated changes in one approval.
5. Do not delete advertising objects in version 0.1.
6. Do not change billing, access, pixels, datasets or customer lists.
7. Do not log secrets or personal lead data.
8. Do not claim causality from platform correlation alone.
9. Do not use a fixed frequency cap without context.
10. Do not optimize for lead volume while ignoring quality and follow-up.
