# Lead Generation Campaign Builder

## Workflow

1. Collect or infer the campaign brief using the fields in `references/input-contract.md`.
2. Mark material unknowns. Do not hide missing commercial or operational inputs.
3. Confirm the conversion location and the primary business outcome.
4. Design the simplest structure that can answer the stated hypotheses.
5. Define audience controls, exclusions and operational constraints.
6. Create the creative matrix and preserve a control variant.
7. Define measurement, CRM feedback and follow-up ownership.
8. Produce an implementation plan in the format below.
9. Create change plans for write actions, but do not execute them without the change-review skill and configured approval.

## Rules

- Optimize for the stated business outcome, not lead volume alone.
- Treat follow-up speed and sales capacity as campaign constraints.
- Avoid unnecessary campaign and ad set fragmentation.
- Keep audience, budget and creative hypotheses distinguishable.
- Do not invent platform capabilities. Ask the connected tool for supported fields and objectives.
- Create new production objects as paused until publication is approved.
- State legal, sector and claim risks.

## Output

Use this structure:

```text
# Campaign plan
## Commercial objective
## Assumptions and missing inputs
## Campaign architecture
## Audience and exclusions
## Offer and conversion location
## Creative matrix
## Budget and pacing
## Measurement and lead quality
## Follow-up operations
## Test and decision plan
## Safety and approvals
## Proposed change plans
```

Read `references/input-contract.md` and `references/campaign-plan-template.md` when building a full plan.
