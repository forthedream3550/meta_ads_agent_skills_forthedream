# State model

- `insufficient_evidence`: missing comparison signals, short window or low delivery.
- `underexposed`: limited repetition and no evidence of saturation.
- `productive_repetition`: frequency rises while response and cost remain stable.
- `watch`: one signal weakens without a consistent pattern.
- `creative_fatigue_likely`: frequency rises, response weakens and cost worsens while new reach slows.
- `audience_saturation_likely`: several creatives weaken together and delivery concentrates on the same audience.

Local thresholds may trigger review, but do not replace interpretation.
