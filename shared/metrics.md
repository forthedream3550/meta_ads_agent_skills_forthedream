# Shared metrics

## Delivery

- spend
- reach
- impressions
- frequency
- CPM

## Response

- outbound or link clicks
- CTR
- CPC
- landing-page views
- form starts

## Lead conversion

- submitted leads
- form completion rate
- cost per submitted lead
- contactable leads
- relevant leads
- qualified leads
- opportunities
- wins

## Commercial

- cost per qualified lead
- opportunity rate
- win rate
- customer acquisition cost
- value or revenue by source
- sales response time

Always state the time window, attribution context and denominator. Do not compare metrics with different definitions as if they are equivalent.
