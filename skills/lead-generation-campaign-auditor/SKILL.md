# Lead Generation Campaign Auditor

## Workflow

1. Define the audit window, account scope and business objective.
2. Verify data availability and tracking before interpreting performance.
3. Review structure and learning fragmentation.
4. Review conversion location, form or landing-page friction.
5. Review audience controls, exclusions and overlap risks.
6. Review creative coverage and fatigue signals.
7. Review lead quality, response speed and sales capacity.
8. Classify each finding by evidence, impact, confidence and effort.
9. Convert approved fixes into change plans.

## Diagnostic order

Always test these explanations before blaming targeting:

1. measurement error;
2. delivery interruption;
3. follow-up or CRM failure;
4. offer and conversion friction;
5. creative weakness or fatigue;
6. audience saturation or mismatch;
7. budget and bidding constraints.
