# Naming conventions

Use readable names that remain useful outside Ads Manager.

Recommended pattern:

```text
[market]_[objective]_[offer]_[audience]_[period]_[variant]
```

Example:

```text
be_leads_consultation_broad_2026q3_video-a
```

Rules:

- use lowercase kebab or underscore style consistently;
- avoid customer personal data;
- include objective and offer;
- identify a creative variant without embedding performance claims;
- do not rename live objects without a clear operational reason;
- preserve provider identifiers in reports and audit logs.
