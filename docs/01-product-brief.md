# Product brief

## Problem

Advertising teams lose time to recurring exports, repetitive account checks and low-value reporting. Raw automation solves part of that problem, but write access introduces financial, operational and reputational risk. A useful agent therefore needs more than API access. It needs a consistent method, commercial context and enforceable boundaries.

## Product definition

For the Dream Meta Ads Skills is a public operating layer for AI-assisted lead generation. It gives agents reusable reasoning workflows and gives teams a policy-controlled MCP layer for data access and limited mutations.

## Primary users

- entrepreneurs who manage meaningful paid-social spend;
- in-house marketing teams;
- performance marketers;
- communication agencies that combine brand and lead generation;
- developers building agentic advertising workflows.

## Jobs to be done

1. Translate a commercial objective into a structured lead campaign.
2. Detect structural, tracking and creative problems before spend increases.
3. Interpret performance instead of restating dashboard metrics.
4. Connect lead quality and sales outcomes to media decisions.
5. Build recognition through controlled repetition.
6. Prevent fatigue through evidence-based creative rotation.
7. Prepare, approve, execute and log narrow campaign changes.

## Non-goals for version 0.1

- fully autonomous account management;
- universal benchmark recommendations;
- billing or payment changes;
- deleting campaigns, audiences or data sources;
- storing personally identifiable lead data;
- replacing strategic positioning, creative direction or sales judgment.

## Product principles

### Commercial outcome before platform metric

A cheap lead is not automatically a valuable lead. Evaluate quality, response speed, opportunity creation and revenue where data exists.

### Evidence before action

Every recommendation must cite the observations that support it and identify missing evidence.

### Reversible changes first

Prefer changes that can be rolled back quickly and evaluated against a baseline.

### Brand memory and activation can coexist

Lead campaigns may create demand and capture demand. Frequency must be interpreted in context, not treated as a single universal ceiling.

### Human control remains explicit

The agent may prepare and execute narrowly scoped changes, but policy and approval remain external to the model.
