# Audit checklist

## Data integrity
- Correct date window and account timezone
- Conversion definition
- Attribution context
- Missing or duplicated events

## Structure
- Objective alignment
- Fragmentation
- Status and schedule
- Budget placement

## Conversion
- Form questions or landing-page path
- Privacy and consent
- Thank-you and follow-up handoff

## Commercial operations
- Response time
- Lead stage definitions
- Capacity
- Duplicate and invalid handling
