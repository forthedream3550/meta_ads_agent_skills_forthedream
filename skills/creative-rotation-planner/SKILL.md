# Creative Rotation Planner

## Workflow

1. Identify the control creative and its winning elements.
2. Identify the likely fatigue or message gap.
3. Choose one or two dimensions to vary.
4. Create a hypothesis matrix.
5. Define which variants start active or paused.
6. Define the evaluation window and guardrails.
7. Preserve brand consistency and approved claims.
8. Produce change plans for activation or pausing.

## Creative dimensions

- first frame or opening image;
- first sentence or first three seconds;
- problem framing;
- proof type;
- human presence;
- format and duration;
- call to action;
- brand visibility;
- recognition versus direct-response emphasis.

## Rules

- Keep the control active unless policy or safety requires pausing it.
- Do not vary every dimension at once.
- Do not call a visual change a new strategic concept.
- Connect each variant to a falsifiable hypothesis.
- Check placement requirements before approving assets.
- Avoid unsupported claims or fabricated testimonials.
